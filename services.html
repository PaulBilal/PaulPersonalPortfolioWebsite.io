Interests

01

Agripreneur

I specialise in rabbit breeding. I also grow vegetables which I sell to the informal market, although I have intentions of supplying big markets some day. I love agriculture as a whole, and I strongly believe agriculture is one industry that will never perish because as people we need to consume food daily. There are a lot of job creation opportunities in the agricultural space. I supply rabbit manure fertilizer which is high in Nitrogen and has an NPK of 2:1:1.


I have intentions of venturing more into meat production and gaining the knowlwdge required. I also plan to gain and apply knowledge of soil preservation, crop production, as well as how to counter act naturally occuring enviromental issues such as soil erosion. As a small scale farmer, I care for the soil and encourage taking care of the soil, not only for us but for fututre generations as well, and that being said, I strongly encourage other farmers as well to practice organic farming, which is good for the soil, and preserves it.


As mentioned before, there is definitely a link between technology and agriculture, and I aim to utilise my skill sets, and natural intuition to apply my knowledge and make the farming journey easier for me and those around me.

So with more incoming data and algorithms improving, blockchain technology is definitely a space we are still going to experience more on. Blockchain technology is not only utilised in crypto currencies and NFTs, but can also be used for security because it is as if it's impenetrable

03

Robotics

I have a deep passion for robotics engineering. It's a path I have been fascinated with from a very young age. I believe it would be a really good initiative that robotics and coding be introduced to the basic education syllabus because it exercises the brain in a very crucial way. Robotics also plays an enormous role in modern day businesses. 04

Marathon Runner

I run atleast 4 times a week. I run because I find joy in running, and also because it is a healthy habit and it keeps the brain awake. I started my running journey in about 2019, but not professionally running, just exercising the body here and there. At the back of my mind, I always had intetions of taking it serious, and it was only in 2023 that I decided to actually take it serious and go compete in marathons.


The longest distance I have ran so far is 32km, and I aim to complete atleast 70km by the end of 2024, as I will be enrolling for the Comrades Marathon for the first time in 2025. So I definitely love running, the feeling one gets after running, the fantastic people one comes across on the running journey, as well as the amount of opportunities that present themselves at such gatherings.


I have ambitions of participating in international marathons in places such as Italy, Turkey, Netherlands, and so forth. I believe the experience would be good for me because I would be interacting with people from different places and learning their different ways of doing things.

05

Photographer

I view photography as a method of relieving myself from work and all other things. I have been shooting pictures of objects since my early high school days, and that is where the love of photography came from. I take pictures of objects and focus on the details of the object rather than taking pictures of people.


When I do not have the motivation to code, I simply take my camera and go around in the yard and finding objects to capture. Being out in the open like that refreshes my mind and then I can go back to work with an open mind.

Agriculture and Technology

Agriculture and technology go hand in hand. Things like drone technology are being utilised in the farming industry to capture data, get aerial photographs, and many more reasons. Technology definitely makes life easier for farmers and saves time and money, which is the end goal of every farmer.


Technology offers various farming solutions like bracelets which monitor a farm worker's location while at work, as well as their coming in to work as well as their depature. such technologies are helpful because they can keep track of employee daily activity and use the data to put it back into their business for more informed business decions.
